Support

OptiMAS is in constant development, which means that bugs and problems are bound to pop up. Please let us know what troubles you have and we'll do our best to fix them. Also, please feel free to let us know what additional features and adjustments would be useful in OptiMAS.

All comments, suggestions and complaints should be directed to fvalente@moulon.inra.fr.
